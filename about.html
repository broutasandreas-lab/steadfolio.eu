About Steadfolio

Built for the moment before you feel ready.

Steadfolio exists because most people don't avoid investing out of laziness - they avoid it because nothing out there was built for exactly where they're starting from.

Steadfolio started from a simple, personal frustration: everywhere I looked for a place to learn investing, I found either oversimplified social media advice or dense, jargon-heavy content that assumed I already knew the basics.

There wasn't a middle ground - something that met me where I actually was, went at my pace, and never made me feel behind. So I built the one I wished existed.

Sofia, the AI coach inside Steadfolio, isn't designed to sell you anything or tell you what to buy. It's designed to do one job well: help you actually understand how investing works, structured step by step, so that whatever you decide to do next, you decide it with real understanding behind it.

- Built by people, for people who felt lost investing, too.
