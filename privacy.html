Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This is a draft template pending final legal review, published to be transparent with early users while Steadfolio is in its testing phase. It will be formally finalized before any paid subscription is introduced.

1. Who we are

Steadfolio is currently operated by an individual based in Greece, ahead of formal business registration. For any privacy-related questions, contact [contact email placeholder - to be added].

2. What information we collect

  • Account information: your email address and password (stored securely, never in plain text) when you sign up.
  • Profile information: details you choose to share with Sofia to personalize your learning experience (for example, your starting knowledge level).
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app, such as lessons completed, to track your progress.
  • Conversation content: messages you send to Sofia, in order to generate responses and maintain conversation context.

We do not currently use advertising trackers, third-party analytics, or marketing pixels on the Steadfolio marketing site.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and personalize the Steadfolio learning experience.
  • To save your progress across sessions.
  • To maintain the security of your account.
  • To communicate with you about your account, if needed.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Who processes your data on our behalf

To operate Steadfolio, we rely on a small number of infrastructure providers who process data as part of delivering the service:

  • Database and authentication hosting - used to securely store account and progress data.
  • AI model provider - used to generate Sofia's responses to your messages.
  • Cloud hosting - used to run the application itself.

These providers process data only as necessary to deliver the service, under their own applicable data protection agreements.

5. Cookies

The Steadfolio marketing website does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. The application itself may use essential, functional cookies strictly necessary to keep you securely logged in.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you're located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact [contact email placeholder - to be added].

7. Data retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you'd like your account and associated data deleted, contact us and we'll process the request within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Data security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted storage of credentials. No system is perfectly secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

9. Children's privacy

Steadfolio is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "last updated" date.
